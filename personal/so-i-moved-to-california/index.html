So I Moved to California

June 2nd, 2025

The first in a series (hopefully) of short journal entries

I started a new job in South Bay and finally escaped from Ottawa

Things that have happened to me/things I’ve noticed in my first month here

  • witnessed at least 4 people with the exact same shirt and one guy with the same shirt AND pants combo in the span of 30 minutes
    • I need new clothes
      • I need to stop shopping at Uniqlo
        • at least I need to stop buying ALL my clothes from Uniqlo
  • got taken to eat pho in the hood
    • I watched an insane amount of people getting arrested from the window of my Uber
      • and then my Uber stopped and let me out right in front of the altercation
      • I also saw someone huffing Galaxy Gas for the first time
  • almost got mauled by a dog on an SF bus at 10 pm at night after an One Okay Rock concert
    • we got on the bus and sat down without realizing there was a scruffy looking dude with his giant dog sat across from us
    • one of my friends was smart and noticed the dog so he stood at a safe distance and took photos
    • his girlfriend did not give a fuck and sat down next to the dog
    • myself and my other friend were incredibly locked in the entire bus ride trying not to draw aggro from the dog as it kept barking
      • the owner tried his best to calm the dog down and at one point put his fist in the dog’s mouth to try to calm it down
    • after the ride was over the friend sitting next to me told me he was staring the dog in the eyes the ENTIRE RIDE
      • this makes a lot more sense as to why the dog was so pissed off for the entire duration of the ride
      • this friend needs to be studied
  • that same night i had the most amazing donuts at a 24 hr donut shop
    • 24 hr donuts is a new concept to me
      • the name of the place was Bob’s Donut
  • Waymo is kind of insane in SF and i would recommend it over taking Ubers
    • if only Waymo didn’t cost 2x the price of Uber
  • the drivers here are really crazy
    • within 48 hours of landing we almost got hit by a driver taking a left turn on a red
    • i have witnessed at least half a dozen instances of drivers cutting the triangle because they were stuck on the lane leading to the off ramp
  • in the same vein of thought, I noticed a stunning mentality difference when it comes to car purchases
    • people in America (this is a sweeping generalization) often don’t care about fuel economy or utility as the first priority when buying a car
    • they care more about whether the car will help them survive in an accident
    • this is because they have come the to conclusion that they WILL get into an accident at some point and they want to keep living
    • I’ve seen single people who don’t haul stuff drive massive SUVs solely because everyone else has a massive car too and they don’t want to be stuck holding the short end of the stick when a car runs a red and t-bones them
  • I am going to need to go to gym if I keep eating so much free food
  • my sleep schedule fixed itself
    • I used to sleep at 3 am and wake up at like 11 am while I was on EST
    • now I sleep at 12 and wake up at 8 which is perfect
    • its been over a month and my body still hasn’t revered back to the shitty sleep so maybe I’m fixed
      • either that or the blackout curtains I had in my room at home were destroying my circadian rhythm
  • the weather here is incredibly foreign
    • what do you mean that for the next 8 months there will be no rain at all and every day will be the exact same weather
